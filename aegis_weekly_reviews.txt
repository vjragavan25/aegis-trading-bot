AEGIS WEEKLY REVIEWS
====================
30-minute structured review every Sunday.
Agenda defined in AEGIS_PSYCHOLOGY_PROTOCOL.md (Rule 4).
Format: date, then three parts (Trade Outcomes / Signal Quality / System Health),
then one-paragraph summary note. Keep it honest, keep it brief.

Do NOT skip weeks even if nothing traded. "Nothing traded, market bearish,
system held correctly" is a valid and useful entry.
